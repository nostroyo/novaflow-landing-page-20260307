    Rules in this MVP

    Booster cards

    You hold exactly 3 cards total for the race. Each turn you may play 1 card (+1 or +2), if still available.

    Car stats model

    Every car has straight, curve, and reliability. Straight/curve are car traits, reliability is pilot consistency.

    Turn resolution

    Movement score = focused stat (straight or curve) + booster used (+ reliability influence only via variance).

    Move up/down behavior

    Cars are ranked by cumulative movement. Close gaps + stronger turn momentum trigger positional swings.
